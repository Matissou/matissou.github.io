Few photos from Göteborg, Sweden

Göteborg is the second biggest city in Sweden, with approxmatly half a million of citizens. It a humand sized city, with an archipelago and is more familial than Stockholm from my point of view. Their are many green places, like Botaniska garden (see photos), the natural reserve and all the garden accross the city. It’s not as much pricy as we could think ; food, transports and daily stuff (like coffee, beer …) are like in France, but housing is really expensive and hard to find. It’s a “student” city because of the two main institution : GU (Göteborg University) and Chalmers (Engineering school of technology). I’m studying at Chalmers and it’s a really big campus, comparing to France : their are pubs, shops, coffee places and even music/clubs inside the campus. Like a town in another.
